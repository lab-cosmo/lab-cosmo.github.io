Code repositories from the Laboratory of Computational Science and Modelling at EPFL

This page is just an index of the documentation and project websites for the software repositories developed by the Laboratory of Computational Science and Modeling (COSMO). You can visit our homepage to get an idea of the kind of research we do at COSMO, and visit our Github organization page to see all the different open repositories that are available, some of which do not provide a dedicated website.
